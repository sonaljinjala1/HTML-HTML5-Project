The National History Museum Ice Rink in London Whether you’re looking for a kid-friendly activity in London or planning a romantic outing with your special someone, a 50-minute skating session at the Natural History Museum Ice Rink is sure to do the trick. Open from 9am to 10pm, it’s easy to fit the wintertime event into any schedule. Ice skates and admission to the museum are also included with your purchase. Visit the museum when it’s convenient for you Ice skate in an iconic London location Enjoy a fun activity with the whole family Take advantage of the city’s winter weather The museum is home to life and earth science specimens comprising some 80 million items within five main collections: botany, entomology, mineralogy, palaeontology and zoology. The museum is a centre of research specialising in taxonomy, identification and conservation.

Big Ben Clock Tower in London, England:

What is the Big Ben in London about? Big Ben - UK Parliament Big Ben is probably the world's most famous clock. That iconic silhouette is instantly recognisable and is one of the most Instagrammed landmarks on the planet. Six monarchs and 41 prime ministers have come and gone since the bells first struck their now familiar music across Westminster.
